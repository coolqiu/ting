# 🚀 Getting Started

Welcome to the world of Ting! Before you dive into your studies, let's set up your learning environment.

## 1. Login and Registration
When you launch the app for the first time, you'll need to create a local learning account. This helps the system track your progress, dictation accuracy, and shadowing scores.

*   **Create Account**: Click the **"Register"** button on the login screen. Enter your username and set a simple password.
*   **Local Storage**: Rest assured, your account data and study records are stored entirely on your local computer and are never uploaded to any server.

## 2. Managing Users
Ting supports multiple users sharing the same application.
*   **Multi-user Mode**: On the launch screen or after logging out, you can click different user avatars to enter their respective study spaces.
*   **Personalization**: Each user has an independent library and detailed study statistics.
