# ✍️ Practice Features

Ting is more than just a player; it includes deep training tools for both input and output.

## 1. Dictation Practice
Want to test your hearing?
- **Real-time Dictation**: Expand the dictation panel at the bottom of the workspace and type what you hear.
- **Smart Scoring**: After submitting, Ting compares your text with the standard transcript, highlights errors in different colors, and gives you a percentage score.

## 2. Shadowing and AI Evaluation (Speaking)
This is Ting's core advantage:
- **Record and Repeat**: Select a sentence, click the record button, and mimic the original voice.
- **Phoneme-level AI Analysis**: Once you finish recording, the system analyzes every detail of your pronunciation.
- **Visual Feedback**: Using **Red/Green** colors, Ting precisely marks which phonemes were pronounced correctly or incorrectly, allowing you to see your pronunciation flaws like looking at sheet music.
