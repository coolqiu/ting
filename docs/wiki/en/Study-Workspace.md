# 🎧 Study Workspace

The Study Workspace is the heart of Ting, integrating all the tools needed for high-efficiency listening training.

## 1. Auto-Resume
Ting remembers your progress for every piece of material. When you return to the workspace, the system automatically restores:
- Your last playback position.
- Your playback speed setting.
- Your designated AB repeat segments.

## 2. AB Repeat Practice
For difficult parts that you can't quite catch:
- **Mark AB Points**: Click "Add AB Segment" or use hotkeys to mark the start and end.
- **Loop Playback**: Enable loop mode to focus all your energy on mastering a specific sentence.

## 3. Interactive Subtitles
- **Click-to-Play**: Click any word in the subtitle area, and the player will instantly jump to the corresponding timestamp.
- **Select for Translation/Shadowing**: Highlight words or phrases to translate them instantly or lock the range for focused shadowing practice.
