# 📚 Library Management (Library)

Organize all your listening materials effortlessly within Ting's "Library."

## 1. Importing Local Files
If you already have audio files (e.g., MP3, WAV):
1. Click **"Library"** in the left navigation bar.
2. Click **"＋ Add Local File."**
3. Select media files from your computer. The system will automatically add them to your collection.

## 2. URL Import (Web Audio)
Want to study directly from a video or podcast on the web?
1. Click **"🔗 Import URL."**
2. Paste a supported link (e.g., YouTube, Bilibili, and other major platforms).
3. The app will download the audio stream, extract it, and convert it into a learnable format.

## 3. Search and Sort
As your library grows, use the search box at the top to quickly find your materials, or sort them by "Newest" or "Title."
